Activity Nag

Posted on Mon 03 January 2022 in Digital Minimalism

Digital Minimalism

I'm trying out a month of Digital Minimalism and I wanted some… encouragement to ensure I stick to my rules. One of which is to use my laptop only for specific tasks I know ahead of time, for an hour a day only. I currently already use Activitywatch to track all my activities on my machine, so I wanted a quick script that would(after an hour) nag me constantly if I wanted to continue using my machine. So I made ActivityNag!
