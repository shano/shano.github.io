Thanks, that’s great to hear that and very much appreciated. I totally get that meditation can be a confusing sell, probably because the thing it is is so mundane and boring, just focusing on the present sounds like trite advice, but it’s basically the starting point for all those benefits you read about.

For example, I’ve become a lot calmer, not because meditation made me calm but that when thing made me anxious instead of immediately attaching myself to the anxiety, I became aware of ‘oh, I think this is anxiety coming on’, and sitting with it, in awareness, just made me less attached to it and generally calmer. Not always, and not 100% detached from it, but it definitely gave enough space from these types of feelings.

As terrifyingly simple as it sounds, meditation is literally just awareness. For better or worse. :)

Good luck with it!
